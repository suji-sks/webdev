Bridge Reportedly Stolen in Bihar

By: Staff Reporter | September 22, 2025

Dateline: Patna, Bihar

Summary: Local officials say portions of a small rural bridge in Bihar were removed overnight, leaving the structure unsafe and cutting off access for nearby villagers.

What happened

Residents of a village in Bihar discovered on Monday that several metal beams and wooden planks from a modest crossing over a seasonal stream had been taken. The bridge — used daily by farmers and students — is now unsafe for passage.

Official response

The local panchayat secretary told reporters that a complaint has been filed with the police. A police spokesperson confirmed an initial report has been registered and that an inquiry is underway.

Impact

  • Villagers are using longer alternate routes to reach markets and schools.
  • There are concerns about delayed medical access in emergencies.
  • Authorities say temporary repairs will be arranged while an investigation continues.

Next steps

Police investigators will review nearby CCTV footage, if available, and question residents. Local officials plan to prioritize a safe temporary crossing before the monsoon worsens local roads.
